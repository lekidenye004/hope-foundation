About Hope Foundation

Learn about our mission, values, and the team behind the change

Our Mission

To empower communities through sustainable development, education, and healthcare initiatives, creating lasting positive change in the lives of those we serve.

Since our founding in 2010, we've been committed to working alongside local communities to identify their needs and develop solutions that create real, measurable impact. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to live a life of dignity and hope.

Through collaboration with local partners, volunteers, and supporters like you, we've been able to reach over 100,000 people across 15 countries, providing education, healthcare, clean water, and food security programs.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach every challenge with empathy and understanding, treating every individual with dignity and respect.

Integrity

We act with honesty and transparency in all we do, ensuring every donation makes the maximum impact.

Sustainability

We create lasting solutions that communities can maintain long after our programs conclude.

Collaboration

We work together with communities, partners, and supporters to achieve meaningful change.

Our Journey

2010

Hope Foundation was founded with a mission to serve underprivileged communities.

2013

Launched our first Education for All program, building 5 schools in rural Kenya.

2016

Expanded to 10 countries and introduced the Healthcare Access program.

2019

Launched Clean Water Initiative, providing clean water to 20,000+ people.

2024

Reached 100,000+ lives impacted across 15 countries worldwide.
